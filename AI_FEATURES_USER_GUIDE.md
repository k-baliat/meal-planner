## Troubleshooting

### "Chef Coco couldn't extract the recipe"
- The link might not contain a clear recipe
- Try a different source or paste the recipe details manually
- Some websites may have complex formatting

### "Instructions are missing from my old recipes"
- This feature applies to NEW recipes created after the update
- Old recipes won't automatically have instructions added
- You can edit old recipes and ask Chef Coco to add instructions

### "Instructions look messy"
- Make sure you're viewing in the Notes section
- The formatting should show numbered steps
- If it's all on one line, there might be a display issue
